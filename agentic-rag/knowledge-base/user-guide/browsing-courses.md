# Browsing and Discovering Courses

## Overview

This guide helps you find and explore courses on the platform. Learn how to search, filter, and discover courses that match your interests.

## Accessing the Course Catalog

1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to the "Courses" section from the main menu
3. You'll see a list of available courses

## Course Information Display

Each course listing shows:

- **Course Thumbnail**: Visual representation of the course
- **Course Title**: Name of the course
- **Instructor Name**: Who teaches the course
- **Rating**: Average student rating (if available)
- **Price**: Course price or "Free" label
- **Enrollment Count**: Number of students enrolled
- **Tags**: Topics and skills covered

## Searching for Courses

### Basic Search

1. Use the search bar at the top of the course catalog
2. Enter keywords related to what you want to learn
3. Press Enter or click the search icon
4. Results will filter based on your search terms

### Search Tips

- Use specific terms: "React" instead of "programming"
- Try different keywords if initial search doesn't yield results
- Search by skill, tool, or topic name
- Combine multiple keywords for better results

## Filtering Courses

### By Tags

1. Click on any tag displayed on a course card
2. You'll see all courses with that tag
3. Use multiple tags to narrow down results

### By Category

- Browse courses by subject categories
- Click on category names to filter
- Categories help organize courses by field

### By Price

- Filter to show only free courses
- Filter to show only paid courses
- Or view all courses regardless of price

## Viewing Course Details

### Course Detail Page

Click on any course to see:

1. **Landing Page**: Course title, description, instructor info
2. **Course Content**: List of chapters and lessons
3. **Reviews**: Student ratings and feedback
4. **Instructor Information**: About the teacher
5. **Pricing**: Course price and enrollment options

### Course Preview

- Watch the promotional video (if available)
- Read the full course description
- Review learning objectives
- Check course requirements and prerequisites
- See what students are saying in reviews

## Course Recommendations

The platform may show:

- **Popular Courses**: Trending courses with many enrollments
- **Recommended for You**: Based on your interests and previous enrollments
- **Similar Courses**: Related to courses you've viewed
- **New Courses**: Recently published courses

## Saving Courses

### Wishlist (if available)

1. Click the bookmark/save icon on course cards
2. Access saved courses from your profile
3. Return to them later for enrollment

## Best Practices for Course Discovery

### Define Your Learning Goals

- Know what skills you want to learn
- Identify your current skill level
- Set learning objectives

### Read Reviews

- Check student ratings and reviews
- Look for detailed feedback about course quality
- Consider both positive and negative reviews
- Pay attention to reviews from students at your level

### Check Course Content

- Review the course curriculum
- Ensure topics match your learning goals
- Check lesson count and estimated duration
- Verify prerequisites are met

### Compare Options

- Look at multiple courses on the same topic
- Compare pricing, content, and instructor credentials
- Read different course descriptions
- Check instructor's other courses

## Tips for Finding the Right Course

1. **Be Specific**: Narrow down your search to specific topics
2. **Check Prerequisites**: Ensure you meet course requirements
3. **Read Descriptions**: Understand what you'll learn
4. **Review Ratings**: Look for courses with good student feedback
5. **Consider Your Level**: Choose courses appropriate for your skill level
6. **Time Commitment**: Check course length and your availability

## Getting Help

If you can't find what you're looking for:

- Try different search terms
- Browse by categories or tags
- Check if courses are available in your preferred language
- Contact support for assistance
