# Enrolling in Courses

## Overview

This guide explains how to enroll in courses on the platform, including both free and paid courses, and what happens after enrollment.

## Prerequisites

Before enrolling:

1. **Create an Account**: You must have a registered account
2. **Log In**: Ensure you're logged in to your account
3. **View Course**: Navigate to the course detail page

## Enrolling in Free Courses

### Steps

1. Navigate to the course detail page
2. Review course information, content, and reviews
3. Click the "Enroll" or "Enroll Now" button
4. You'll be immediately enrolled in the course
5. You can start learning right away

### What Happens After Enrollment

- Course appears in "My Learning" section
- You gain access to all course content
- Can start lessons immediately
- Progress is tracked automatically

## Enrolling in Paid Courses

### Steps

1. Navigate to the course detail page
2. Review course information carefully
3. Click "Enroll" or "Buy Now" button
4. You'll be redirected to the payment page
5. Complete the payment process using available payment methods
6. After successful payment, you'll be enrolled automatically

### Payment Process

1. **Select Payment Method**: Choose your preferred payment option
2. **Enter Payment Details**: Provide required payment information
3. **Review Order**: Confirm course and price
4. **Complete Payment**: Submit payment
5. **Confirmation**: Receive enrollment confirmation

### Payment Methods

Available payment methods may include:
- Credit/Debit cards
- Digital wallets
- Bank transfers
- Other platform-supported methods

## Course Access After Enrollment

### Immediate Access

Once enrolled, you can:

- Access all course chapters and lessons
- Watch videos, read articles, take quizzes
- Download course materials and attachments
- Track your learning progress
- Participate in course discussions (if available)
- Leave reviews and ratings

### Course Progress

- Your progress is automatically saved
- You can resume from where you left off
- Completed lessons are marked
- Overall course completion percentage is tracked

## Managing Enrollments

### Viewing Your Courses

1. Navigate to "My Learning" from the main menu
2. See all enrolled courses
3. Filter by status (In Progress, Completed, etc.)
4. Click on any course to continue learning

### Course Status

Courses can show different statuses:

- **In Progress**: Currently learning
- **Not Started**: Enrolled but haven't begun
- **Completed**: Finished all lessons
- **Archived**: Moved to archive (if available)

## Important Notes

### Enrollment Policies

- **Lifetime Access**: Once enrolled, you typically have lifetime access (check platform policy)
- **No Refunds**: Review refund policy before purchasing paid courses
- **Course Updates**: You'll receive access to course updates automatically
- **Mobile Access**: Courses are accessible on various devices

### Enrollment Limitations

- Some courses may have enrollment deadlines
- Limited seats may apply to certain courses
- Prerequisites may be required for advanced courses

## Troubleshooting Enrollment Issues

### Cannot Enroll in Free Course

If you're having trouble enrolling:

1. Ensure you're logged in
2. Check if you're already enrolled
3. Refresh the page and try again
4. Clear browser cache if issues persist
5. Contact support if problem continues

### Payment Issues

If payment fails:

1. Verify payment method details
2. Check account balance or card limits
3. Try a different payment method
4. Ensure internet connection is stable
5. Contact payment support if needed

### Access Issues After Enrollment

If you can't access course content:

1. Verify enrollment was successful
2. Check "My Learning" section
3. Refresh the page
4. Log out and log back in
5. Contact support with enrollment confirmation

## Best Practices

### Before Enrolling

1. **Read Course Description**: Understand what you'll learn
2. **Check Prerequisites**: Ensure you meet requirements
3. **Review Curriculum**: See course structure and content
4. **Read Reviews**: Learn from other students' experiences
5. **Check Instructor**: Review instructor credentials

### After Enrolling

1. **Start Learning**: Begin with the first lesson
2. **Set Goals**: Define what you want to achieve
3. **Stay Consistent**: Regular study improves retention
4. **Take Notes**: Document key learnings
5. **Practice**: Apply what you learn
6. **Engage**: Participate in discussions and ask questions

## Getting the Most from Your Enrollment

- **Complete Lessons**: Finish all course content
- **Practice Regularly**: Consistent practice improves learning
- **Ask Questions**: Use Q&A or discussion features
- **Review Material**: Revisit lessons as needed
- **Apply Learning**: Use new skills in real projects
- **Leave Feedback**: Help improve courses with reviews
