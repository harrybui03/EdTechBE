# Creating a New Course

## Overview

This guide will help you create a new course on the platform. The course creation process consists of three main steps.

## Step 1: Access Course Creation

1. Log in and navigate to the Instructor Dashboard
2. Click on "Courses" in the navigation menu
3. Select "Create New Course"

## Step 2: Complete the Course Creation Wizard

The course creation wizard has three steps:

### Step 1: Basic Information

- **Course Title**: Enter a working title for your course (minimum 10 characters)
  - Example: "Learn Photoshop CS6 from scratch"
  - You can change this later if needed
  - The title field is required and will be validated

### Step 2: Tags and Labels

- **Tags**: Add relevant tags to help students discover your course
- **Labels**: Add labels to categorize your course
- These help with course discoverability and organization

### Step 3: Time Information

- Set the course duration and timing details
- This information helps students understand the course structure

## Step 3: Create the Course

1. Review all information entered in the three steps
2. Click "Create Course" on the final step
3. You'll be redirected to the course editing page after successful creation

## Important Notes

- After creation, your course will be in "Draft" status
- You must add at least one chapter and one lesson before you can publish
- You can always go back and edit course information later
- Use the "Exit" button to cancel the creation process at any time

## Next Steps

After creating your course:

1. Add course structure (chapters and lessons)
2. Create course content (videos, articles, quizzes)
3. Set up the landing page
4. Configure pricing
5. Publish your course
