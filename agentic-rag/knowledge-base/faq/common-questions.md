# Common Questions and Answers

## General Questions

### How do I create an account?

1. Click "Sign Up" or "Register" on the homepage
2. Enter your email address and create a password
3. Verify your email address (check your inbox)
4. Complete your profile information
5. Start browsing and enrolling in courses

### How do I reset my password?

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

### Is the platform free to use?

- Creating an account is free
- Browsing courses is free
- Some courses are free to enroll
- Paid courses require payment before enrollment
- Check individual course pricing

## Course Enrollment Questions

### Can I enroll in multiple courses?

Yes, you can enroll in as many courses as you want. There's no limit on the number of courses you can take.

### Do I have lifetime access to enrolled courses?

Typically, yes. Once you enroll in a course (free or paid), you usually have lifetime access to the course content. However, check the specific course details for any limitations.

### Can I get a refund for a paid course?

Refund policies vary. Check the platform's refund policy before purchasing. Generally:
- Review course information carefully before buying
- Contact support if you have issues
- Some courses may offer money-back guarantees

### What happens if I don't finish a course?

- Your progress is saved
- You can return anytime to continue
- No penalty for incomplete courses
- You can restart from where you left off

## Learning Questions

### How long does it take to complete a course?

Course duration varies:
- Check course description for estimated time
- Depends on course length and your pace
- Some courses can be completed in hours, others in weeks
- Learn at your own pace

### Can I learn on mobile devices?

Yes, the platform is mobile-responsive:

- Use mobile browsers to learn
- Video playback works on mobile
- Download materials for offline access (if available)

### How do I track my progress?

- Progress is automatically tracked
- See completion percentage on course page
- Check "My Learning" for overall progress
- Completed lessons are marked with checkmarks

### Can I download course materials?

Some courses include downloadable materials:
- Look for download icons on lessons
- Files may include PDFs, code, resources
- Download and save for offline access
- Organize files for easy reference

## Instructor Questions

### How do I become an instructor?

1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to "Become an Instructor"
3. Click "Get Started"
4. System will assign you instructor role
5. Access instructor dashboard

### What are the requirements to publish a course?

Before publishing, ensure:
- At least 1 chapter in your course
- At least 1 lesson in your course
- Complete course information filled out
- Landing page configured
- Pricing set (can be free)

### How do I set course pricing?

1. Go to course editing page
2. Navigate to "Pricing" section
3. Toggle "Paid Course" if charging
4. Set original price and selling price
5. Select currency
6. Save changes

### Can I edit my course after publishing?

Yes, you can edit course content after publishing:
- Update lessons and chapters
- Modify course information
- Change pricing
- Add new content
- Students get access to updates

## Technical Questions

### Videos won't play. What should I do?

Try these solutions:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Check if video format is supported
6. Disable browser extensions
7. Contact support if issue persists

### The page is loading slowly. Why?

Possible causes:
- Slow internet connection
- High traffic on platform
- Browser issues
- Device performance

Solutions:
- Check internet speed
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache
- Close other browser tabs
- Try a different browser

### Can I use the platform offline?

Limited offline access:
- Some downloaded materials work offline
- Videos typically require internet
- Quiz submissions need connection
- Best to use with stable internet

### Which browsers are supported?

The platform works best on:
- Chrome (latest version)
- Firefox (latest version)
- Safari (latest version)
- Edge (latest version)

Update your browser for best experience.
